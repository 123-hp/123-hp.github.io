HP Officejet Pro 6960 Google Cloud Print Setup

The features provided below are common for the HP Officejet Pro range of printers like, Officejet Pro 6960, Officejet Pro 6860, Officejet Pro 6960,Officejet Pro 6968, Officejet Pro 6970, Officejet Pro 6970, Officejet Pro 8600, Officejet Pro 8660, Officejet Pro 8740…etc.,

HP Officejet Pro 6960 Google Cloud Print Setup is a service that enables you to print photos and documents securely from any network-connected device (like a phone, computer or tablet) or any mobile device to your HP Officejet Pro 6960.

Step 1 – Registering your Google Cloud Print Ready printer

Please note that Google Cloud Print does not work when your Officejet Pro 6960 is connected through a USB cable.

  1. Switch on your Officejet Pro 6960.
  2. Sign into Google from the Chrome browser .
  3. Click on the Chrome menu icon and then choose Settings from the drop-down list.
  4. From the Settings window, navigate down and choose Show Advanced Settings.
  5. Navigate down to Google Cloud Print and then click on the Manage button.
  6. HP Officejet Pro 6960 Google Cloud Print Setup will automatically detect any printer on the network,

Step 2 – Printing from your mobile device or computer

Printing an image or document saved on the computer

  1. Sign into Google from your Chrome browser.
  2. Navigate to Google Cloud Printjobs.
  3. Click on Print, choose Upload file to print and then click on Select a file from my computer.
  4. Choose the document that you would like to print and then click on Open.
  5. Choose your printer from the Google Cloud Print Window and then click on Print.
  6. Modify print settings if required and then click on Print again.
  • If your Officejet Pro 6960 is shown under New devices, click on Register and then click on Register again to provide confirmation and get a confirmation page printed.
  • If the printer is shown under My devices, then it means that the printer is already registered with HP Officejet Pro 6960 Google Cloud Print Setup. Click on Manage to get your registered Google Cloud Print printers displayed.
  • If your Officejet Pro 6960 is not shown, ensure that your printer is connected to the network. Also, try connecting with the HP Officejet Pro 6960’s email address.

Printing an document, image, email or web page from Android 4.4 and later mobile device (tablets and smartphones)

  • Google Play Store should be opened.
  • Do a search for Cloud Print and then tap on Cloud Print by Google, Inc.
  • After installing Cloud Print, tap Back .
  • Touch on Settings, touch More settings or More networks and then touch Printing




  • Navigate to Cloud Print and then touch On.
  • Open a saved image or document, or open a web page or an email. Now, touch the menu icon
  • Touch on Print and then touch on Google Print.
  • Choose your printer (HP Officejet Pro 6960), modify print settings if required and then touch Print.

Printing a web page from the chrome app

  • To get started with the Chrome browser click the Chrome app, from your iPad or iPhone.
  • Before clicking the menu icon , select the web page that you need to print.

  • Choose the action icon, click Print,then choose HP Officejet Pro 6960 Google Cloud Print Setup.
  • Choose your printer, alter print settings if required, and then click Print.
