Driver Installation for MacOS

The instructions provided here is for HP Envy 5660 Printers with full feature downloadable drivers for OS X and MacOS. Connect you Envy 5660 and install software and full feature print 123 HP Envy 5660 Driver.

Step 1 – Connecting the HP Envy 5660

When your printer is connected through USB to your computer

  1. Search for printers in your Mac and then click on Print & Scan, Print & Fax or Printers and Scanners in the search results.
  2. Click on Envy 5660 and then click on the minus sign to get it removed from the queue.
  3. Detach the printer USB cable from your Mac.

Preparing for the connection

  1. You should have a switch, hub or network router with Ethernet ports available on them.
  2. Ensure that the computer is connected to the switch, hub or network router.
  3. Please us an Ethernet cable. A standard telephone cable should not be used.



  1. Ethernet cable – Connector will be wide and have eight wires
  2. Telephone cable (should not be used) – Connector will be narrow and will have between two and six wires

Connecting your Envy 5660 to the network

  • Switch on the 123.hp.com/envy5660 printer.
  • Any protective cover or plug should be removed from the Ethernet port. This port will be found in the rear of the printer.
  • One end of the Ethernet cable should be connected to the printer port. Now, connect the other end of the cable to a port on the switch, hub or network router.

123 HP SETUP ENVY 5660 DRIVER INSTALLATION

  • Switch on the printer.
  • If your Envy 5660 is connected through USB to your computer, then detach the USB cable from the computer.
  • Go to HP’s software and 123 HP Envy 5660 Driver download page.
  • When prompted, choose a method to select your printer model and proceed as per the instructions displayed on the screen.
  • Click on Download near HP Easy Start or near the full feature driver (based on the option that is displayed).
  • To begin the installation, open the full feature driver .dmg file or the HP Easy Start file from the Downloads folder.
  • Proceed as per the instructions displayed on the screen to set up the Envy 5660 printer connection and install the 123.hp.com/envy5660 printer software.
  • When the installer prompts, click on Add Printer in order to create a print queue on your Mac.
  • The Add window will open.
  • Click on the name of your printer (HP Envy 5660)and then click on the Print Using or Use menu. Now, choose the name of your printer in the pop-up menu that displays and click on Add.
  • Go back to the HP installer in order to complete the installation process.
